# Inclusion and Exclusion Criteria

## Include

- Studies about Urban Building Energy Modeling (UBEM), urban-scale building
  energy modeling, city-scale building energy simulation, or building stock
  energy modeling.
- Studies that model multiple buildings, districts, neighborhoods, cities, or
  national/regional building stocks when urban energy demand is central.
- Studies that describe methods, input data, validation, uncertainty,
  calibration, retrofit scenarios, policy scenarios, or software workflows for
  UBEM-like analysis.
- Peer-reviewed articles, conference papers, preprints, theses, technical
  reports, or other research outputs when bibliographic metadata is available
  from an accepted source or local BibTeX input.

## Exclude

- Single-building energy simulation papers with no urban, district, stock, or
  city-scale modeling component.
- Papers focused only on urban microclimate, transport, power systems, or
  district energy networks unless building energy demand modeling is explicit.
- Records without a title.
- Records known to come from MDPI or MPDI sources.
- Records whose bibliographic details cannot be traced to OpenAlex, Crossref,
  arXiv, or local BibTeX input.

## Screening Decisions

Use these decision labels:

- `include`: eligible for full-text review or extraction.
- `exclude`: outside scope or excluded source.
- `maybe`: unclear from title and abstract; needs full-text check.
- `duplicate`: duplicate of another retained record.

Do not use an `include` decision unless the decision can be justified from the
record metadata, abstract, full text, or researcher notes.
