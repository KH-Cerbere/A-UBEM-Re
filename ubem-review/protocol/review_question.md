# Review Question

How has Urban Building Energy Modeling (UBEM) been defined, implemented,
validated, and applied for city-scale or urban-scale building energy analysis?

## Sub-Questions

- What modeling approaches are used for UBEM and related city-scale building
  energy simulation?
- Which data sources are used to describe buildings, weather, occupancy,
  systems, and urban context?
- How are UBEM results validated or calibrated?
- Which software tools and simulation engines are commonly used?
- What urban planning, retrofit, climate, resilience, or policy questions are
  addressed?
- What limitations, uncertainties, and reproducibility gaps are reported?

## Unit of Analysis

The primary unit of analysis is a scholarly record, report, preprint, or local
BibTeX entry that explicitly addresses urban-scale or stock-scale building
energy modeling.
